BYTE THE RHYTHM

Event Details

  • Domain: Open
  • Participants: 6 per team
  • Mode: Offline

General Guidelines

  • Participants are to perform a dance on any one of the provided themes.
  • Each team will be given 3.5 minutes to present their dance and 30 seconds for prop placement.
  • Song lyrics with indecent, vulgar, or objectionable language and dance choreography with inappropriate gestures are not allowed.
  • The organizers will only provide a sound system to the participants.
  • Background presentations like PPT and videos are not allowed.
  • Music for the dance must be uploaded through the submission link for the event by 17th July 2025. No alterations will be accepted thereafter.
  • Usage of hand props and costumes are allowed.
  • Only the first 12 entries will be entertained.

Dance Themes

  • 1. INTERNET ADDICTION
  • 2. LIFE REMOTE CONTROL
  • 3. VIRTUAL REALITY WORLD
  • 4. RISE OF AI: TECH VS NATURE

Judgement Criteria

Overall Presentation

Stage presence and performance quality

Relevance to the Theme

Adherence to chosen theme

Wow Factor

Memorable and impactful elements

Originality of Choreography

Creativity in dance moves
