Event Details

  • Domain: VIII - X
  • No. of participants per team: 1
  • Software: Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript (VS CODE WILL BE PROVIDED)
  • Mode: Prelims (online) & finals (offline)

General Guidelines

Prelims

  • Participants will have to create a website using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The topic will be announced on our Discord server and official website.
  • Participants are required to host their websites on a suitable platform such as GitHub Pages or Firebase and submit the URL through a form link, which will be provided at a later date.
  • Use of any AI, pre-installed templates or frameworks are not allowed and will result in immediate disqualification.

Finals

  • The Participants will be given a time bracket of 1 hour to make a website on a topic disclosed on the spot.
  • No external media will be allowed.
  • Images and videos to be used for making the website will be provided.
  • Use of any unfair means will lead to immediate disqualification.
