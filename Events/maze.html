Maze and Mayhem

Event Details

  • Domain: Open
  • No. of participants per team: 2
  • Mode: Offline

Robot Specifications

  • One custom-built robot per team (pre-assembled kits are prohibited).
  • Maximum dimensions: 30cm × 30cm × 30cm.
  • Maximum weight: 3kg.
  • Wheels or tracks only (aerial systems not permitted).
  • Provision for a VIP payload module (10cm × 10cm × 10cm).
  • No destructive or weaponized components.
  • Maintain a concise build log, including key design decisions and component lists (subject to inspection).

Game Play

  • Navigate a challenging course with multiple path options.
  • Encounter dynamic obstacles and a central combat engagement area.
  • Safely transport the VIP payload module towards the finish.
  • Emphasis on strategy, timing, and adaptability (teams may choose different routes and tactics).
  • Exact course layout, scoring methodology, and round structure will be announced at the event briefing and on the official Discord server.

Judgement Criteria

Navigation Proficiency

Ability to efficiently navigate the maze course

Combat Strategy

Effectiveness in combat engagement area

Payload Management

Successful transport of VIP payload module
