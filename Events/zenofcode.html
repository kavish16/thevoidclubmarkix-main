Zen Of Code

Event Details

  • Domain: VIII-X
  • Participants: 2 per team
  • Language: Python (3.7 and above)
  • Duration: 60 + 20 minutes
  • Mode:Offline

General Guidelines

Two Rounds Competition

  • Participants have to compete in two rounds: A pen and paper question round and a final round.
  • The question-answer round consists of logical thinking and general knowledge questions related to Python.

Requirements & Rules

  • Participants are required to have a HackerRank account.
  • The question-answer round and the final round will be used to calculate a team's final score.
  • Computer systems will be provided by the school but an attempt to use unfair means (opening a search engine, ChatGPT, etc.) will result in immediate disqualification.

Judgement Criteria

Logic

Quality and efficiency of the problem-solving approach

Efficiency

Optimization of code performance and resource usage

Readability

Easy to understand
