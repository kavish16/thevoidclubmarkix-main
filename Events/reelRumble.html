Reel Rumble

Event Details

  • Domain: IX - XII
  • Participants: 2 (excluding actors)
  • Software: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Filmora, DaVinci Resolve, Sony Vegas
  • Mode: Prelims (Online) & Finals (Offline)

General Guidelines

  • For the prelims, participants will have to create a teaser. The topic will be announced on our Discord server and official website.
  • The teams have to submit their teasers to us through an email id, which will be provided at a later date.
  • The top 10 teams will qualify for the final event.
  • The selected teams will have to complete the movie extending it to a duration of 4-6 minutes on the selected topic.
  • The teams should bring their movies in a pendrive to the final event.
  • The duration of the stock footages & blanks in the movie shouldn't exceed 20s. AI generated content is limited to 10s.
  • The movie should include a stop motion piece of about 15 seconds.
  • Premade templates are not allowed.
  • The presentation of the movie will be followed by a cross questioning round by the judges.

Judgement Criteria

Originality

Creativity and uniqueness of content

VFX & SFX

Quality of visual and sound effects

Cinematography

Visual storytelling techniques

Story

Narrative quality and structure

Pitching

Presentation of the movie
