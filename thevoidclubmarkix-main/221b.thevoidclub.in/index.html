ABOUT THE HUNT

1. Sherlocked is the annual online cryptic hunt hosted by the VOID club, the official computer club of Somerville School, NOIDA.
2. Sherlocked 2024 shall be a 48 hour hunt, starting at 1200 hours on July 24, 2024 and concluding at 1200 hours on July 26, 2024.
3. There shall be only one winner, who will be school student in the Delhi-NCR region. However, all are free to take part in the hunt.
4. Having a Discord account is must for doing the hunt, and leads and hints shall be shared on the Sherlocked server, and NOT on the Incognito Mark VIII server.
5. Congratulations on finding this website, don't forget it.



RULES

1. Google is your best friend.
2. Anything, we mean anything can be a hint.
3. Answers contain absolutely no space in between the words.
4. Full names when the answer is a person's name.
5. All answers in lowercase and numbers as one, two, etc.
6. All backlinks shall be on 221b.thevoidclub.in
7. In case links don't work, try another browser or device.
