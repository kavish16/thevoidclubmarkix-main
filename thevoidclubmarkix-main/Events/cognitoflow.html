COGNITOFLOW

Event Details

  • Domain: Open
  • Participants: 2 per team
  • Mode: Hybrid (Online Prelims + Offline Finals)

General Guidelines

Prelims

  • Participants must design an innovative solution incorporating AI or other emerging technologies to help people with learning or physical disabilities assimilate into their environment.
  • The solution should aim to increase efficiency and accessibility in their everyday lives.
  • Teams must submit only one deliverable from the following options:
    • 3D Model or Digital Image
    • Promotional Graphics or Explainer Video (45 seconds - 1.5 minutes)
    • Pitch deck or Website/App Mock-up
  • The link and date for submission will be disclosed on the Incognito website.

Finals

  • Shortlisted teams will receive a new topic at the venue and will be provided with the required research material on the spot.
  • They have to fabricate an idea/solution for it, Incorporating AI or other emerging technologies in 45 minutes.
  • Each team will then be given 3 minutes to explain their concept before the judges on the whiteboard or in the form of a PPT/model on the smartboard provided by our school.
  • Use of AI, internet, or any unfair means will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants have to bring their own laptops and will not be allowed to use any other electronics.

Judgement Criteria

Score

Evaluation Point System

Innovation & Uniqueness

Originality of the proposed solution

Feasibility & Practicality

Real-world applicability of the solution

Presentation Quality

Clarity and effectiveness of explanation

Execution

Implementation of technical components
